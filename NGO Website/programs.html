Our Programs

Structured initiatives running under the foundation.

1. Bal Vikas Vatika (Play School)

A neat, child-friendly campus with age-wise activities, stories, basic concepts, prayers and games so that children from rural families get a solid early foundation.

Age group: 2–6 years • Focus: Joyful learning, values & habits.

2. Ramayan Quiz & Value-Education

Multi-round quiz competitions based on Ramayan and other cultural texts to build memory power, reading habit, team spirit and moral understanding.

Rounds: Written, Quick Answer, Buzzer, Visual, Rapid Fire.

3. Yoga & Wellness Classes

Yoga sessions for women, children and youth to improve physical health, mental balance and inner confidence. Also includes basic counselling and motivation.

Proposed centres: Mandhana, Agroha & nearby areas.

4. Support for Weak Learners

Extra-support classes and mentoring for students who are weak in reading, writing or basic concepts – especially in government and rural schools.

5. Women Empowerment & Skill Support

Planned activities include short-term trainings, awareness meetings, legal and health awareness sessions for women and adolescent girls.

6. Community & Village Development

Plantation drives, cleanliness campaigns, awareness on social issues and collaboration with local bodies for better village infrastructure.
