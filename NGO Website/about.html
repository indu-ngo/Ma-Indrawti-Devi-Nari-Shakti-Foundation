About Ma Indrawti Devi Nari Shakti Foundation

Rooted in Mandhana village, dedicated to service and empowerment.

Our Story

Ma Indrawti Devi Nari Shakti Foundation has been started with the inspiration of a mother’s sacrifice, hard work and values. The organisation is based in Damodar Panna, Mandhana, Bhiwani (Haryana) and works primarily with children, women and rural families.

The foundation aims to combine traditional values with modern education – so that every child can read, think and speak with confidence; every woman can stand on her own feet; and every village can move towards self-reliance.

Vision

A strong, educated and value-based rural society where children learn with joy, women feel empowered and respected, and families support each other in times of need.

Mission

To provide accessible education, life-skills, cultural activities, yoga and support programmes for children and women, in partnership with schools, community leaders and well-wishers.

Core Values

  • Seva (Service)
  • Satya & Sadachar (Truth & Integrity)
  • Respect for women & elders
  • Transparency in work and finances

Key Focus Areas

  • Early childhood education (Bal Vikas Vatika / Play School).
  • Educational competitions and quizzes (Ramayan Quiz and similar initiatives).
  • Yoga, health awareness and personality development for children and women.
  • Support classes for academically weaker students in rural areas.
  • Mobilising community for village-level cleanliness, plantation and social harmony.
