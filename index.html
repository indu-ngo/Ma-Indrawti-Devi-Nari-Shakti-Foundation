Building confident children, empowered women & vibrant villages.

Ma Indrawti Devi Nari Shakti Foundation is a community–based NGO in Mandhana (Bhiwani) working for quality education, women empowerment, yoga & holistic rural development.

Flagship Focus Areas

  • Early childhood care & education through Bal Vikas Vatika.
  • Village level competitions like Ramayan Quiz to connect youth with values.
  • Yoga, health awareness & support for women and girls.
  • Support for economically weaker students & families.
We work closely with local schools, gram panchayat and well-wishers to create a strong, compassionate community in and around Mandhana.

What We Do

Key initiatives currently running under Ma Indrawti Devi Nari Shakti Foundation.

Bal Vikas Vatika (Play School)

A child-friendly, safe and loving learning space for children aged 2–6 years with age-appropriate activities, stories, rhymes, play and values education.

Planned at Mandhana, Bhiwani campus.

Educational & Value-Based Competitions

Ramayan Quiz and similar programmes to encourage reading, memory, public speaking and connect children with Indian culture in a joyful way.

First Ramayan Quiz: 30 November 2025, Mandhana.

Yoga & Women Empowerment

Yoga classes, motivation sessions and guidance for women and girls to build confidence, health, skills and self-reliance.

New yoga centres proposed in Agroha & nearby areas.

Recent Highlight

Ramayan Quiz – a collective effort of schools, teachers, students and villagers.
